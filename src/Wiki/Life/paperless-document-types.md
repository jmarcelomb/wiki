### Broad Document Types

1. **Bills**
    * Utility bills (electricity, water, gas)
    * Internet or phone bills
    * Subscription renewals

2. **Receipts**
    * Purchase receipts (groceries, electronics, clothing)
    * Online order confirmations
    * Payment confirmations (e.g., from a creditor or bank)

3. **Financial**
    * Bank statements
    * Investment reports
    * Loan agreements

4. **Insurance**
    * Policies (health, car, home, life)
    * Claim documents

5. **Medical**
    * Doctor's notes
    * Test results
    * Prescription records

6. **Personal Identification**
    * Passports
    * Birth certificates
    * Driver's licenses

7. **Tax**
    * Tax returns
    * W2/1099 forms (or equivalents in your country)
    * Receipts for deductible expenses

8. **Correspondence**
    * Letters
    * Notices
    * Invitations

9. **Education**
    * Certificates
    * Transcripts
    * School correspondence

10. **Legal Documents**
    * Contracts
    * Lease agreements
    * Wills or testaments

11. **Household**
    * Home maintenance records
    * General home-related documents (excluding manuals and warranties)

12. **Travel**
    * Flight itineraries
    * Hotel reservations
    * Visa documents

13. **Work**
    * Pay stubs
    * Employment contracts
    * Performance reviews

14. **Tickets**
    * Concert and event tickets
    * Sporting event confirmations
    * Theater/museum entry passes

15. **Vehicles**
    * Vehicle titles and registration
    * Maintenance and service records
    * Insurance ID cards (for the vehicle)

16. **Manuals & Warranties**
    * Product manuals and guides
    * Warranty cards and extended warranty details
